Lasanga

A delicious lasagna recipe to make

Although the process of making lasagna can take 3 hours, don't let that discourage you. Most of that time is letting the Lasagna bake in the oven. The results will be well worth the wait!

Ingredients

  • lean ground beef and sweet italian sausage
  • Onion
  • garlic (2 cloves)
  • can of crushed tamatoes (1)
  • can of tamato sauce (2)
  • can of tamato paste (2)
  • sugar (2 tbls)
  • fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg (1)

Steps

  1. make the meat sauce
  2. cook teh noodles
  3. make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 50 min (remove foil 25 min in)
  6. Let the lasagna rest for 15 min before serving

How to layer lasagna

layer ingredients in this order:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
