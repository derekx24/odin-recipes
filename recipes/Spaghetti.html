Creamy Scallop Spaghetti

A fast and quick recipe, but most importantly, tastes amazing!

Within 30 minutes, you can make four delicious servings of spaghetti for your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces uncooked thick spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound bay scallops
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons grated lemon zest
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • 1/3 cup dry sherry
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped Italian parsley, divided
  • Freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, for serving

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salt water to boil
  2. Place spaghetti in boiling water, let cook for about 10 min or 1 min less than directed on package (until tender yet firm)
  3. Heat oil in a pan, and let scallops sear for around 1 min each side
  4. add butter and stir until it melts. stir in garlic
  5. add lemon zest and ref pepper flakes
  6. stir in sherry and stir until alcohol cooks off (around 1 min)
  7. Pour in cream. When mixture begins to simmer, reduce heat to medium-low
  8. Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice
  9. Drain pasta and transfer to skillet with scallops. bring to a simmer
  10. Add 1/2 of the chopped parsley. Cook until pasta is heated through and tender (about 1 minute)
  11. Remove from heat. Garnish generously with grated cheese and add the rest of the parsley

Now your ready to dig in and enjoy a nice hot serving of creamy scallop spaghetti!
