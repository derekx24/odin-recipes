Clam Chowder

On a cold winter day, nothing beats a nice and hot clam chowder to warm yourself up!

Within 45 minutes, you can enough serving to feed 8 people!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Clams, or canned minced clams
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cream
  • Red wine vinegar
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the vegetetables in the clam juice from your can of minced clams
  2. Using the butter and flour, make a roux
  3. whisk in the half and half cream
  4. stir in the vegetables you cooked into your roux
  5. add the clams
  6. Once heated, stir in vinegar and seasonings to your liking
  7. you can add in a cornstarch slurry if you prefer thicker soups

Now you are ready to serve and enjoy it with a side of bread or oyster crackers!
